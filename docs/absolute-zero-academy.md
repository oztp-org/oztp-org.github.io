# Absolute Zero Academy

*A program of the Open Zero Trust Project.*

---

Zero Trust is not a product you install. It is a discipline you build — incrementally, continuously, across every layer of your organization. The Absolute Zero Academy exists to teach that discipline: the frameworks, the controls, the architecture decisions, and the judgment that comes from applying them in the real world.

The Academy is built around the same principle as [Absolute Zero](absolute-zero.md) itself. The goal is not to pass a test and stop. The goal is to develop the knowledge and instincts to keep pursuing the ideal — and to help the organizations you work with do the same.

## Three Tiers

The Academy offers three individual certification tiers, each building on the last.

### AZ Foundations

**For:** Security practitioners, IT professionals, and anyone beginning their Zero Trust journey.

**Covers:**

- What Zero Trust is — and why perimeter-based security failed
- The three core tenets of Zero Trust (NIST SP 800-207)
- The five pillars of the CISA Zero Trust Maturity Model
- Introduction to all four major ZT frameworks: NIST, CISA ZTMM, CIS Controls, and ISO 27001
- The OZTP Top 10 Zero Trust Controls
- How to read and act on a ZT maturity assessment
- Building a practical remediation roadmap

**Earns:** AZ Foundations Certificate

[View the AZ Foundations Learning Objectives →](objectives.md) — published as a draft; we're actively looking for practitioner feedback before finalizing.

---

### AZ Practitioner

**For:** Security engineers, architects, and blue team practitioners implementing Zero Trust controls.

**Covers:**

- Deep operational knowledge across all five CISA pillars
- Identity: MFA, privileged access management, just-in-time access, identity lifecycle automation
- Devices: MDM, endpoint detection and response, hardware attestation, device posture enforcement
- Networks: micro-segmentation, Zero Trust Network Access (ZTNA), encrypted traffic inspection
- Applications: least-privilege access, continuous authorization, CI/CD security integration
- Data: classification frameworks, DLP, encryption at rest, in transit, and in use
- CIS Controls IG1 and IG2 in practice
- Running a Zero Trust maturity assessment for an organization
- Incident response within a Zero Trust architecture

**Earns:** AZ Practitioner Certificate

---

### AZ Architect
**For:** Senior security practitioners, security architects, and those building or leading ZT programs — including future Absolute Zero recognition assessors.

**Covers:**
- CISA ZTMM Optimal across all five pillars in practice
- CIS Controls IG3 implementation
- ISO 27001 ISMS design and continuous operation
- Cross-framework gap analysis and multi-year roadmap design
- **Threat Intelligence Integration & Automated Guardrails**
- **Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) Alignment**
- Zero Trust architecture for complex, multi-environment organizations
- Third-party risk and supply chain Zero Trust

**Earns:** AZ Architect Certificate + eligibility to serve as an Absolute Zero recognition assessor

## How It Connects

The Academy and the [Absolute Zero recognition program](absolute-zero.md#the-absolute-zero-recognition-program) are two sides of the same ecosystem. The Academy certifies the individuals; the recognition program certifies the organizations. Architect-level graduates are trained to conduct the evidence reviews that org recognition requires.

| Academy Tier | Supports Org Recognition Tier |
|---|---|
| AZ Foundations | Absolute Zero Foundations |
| AZ Practitioner | Absolute Zero Advanced |
| AZ Architect | Absolute Zero Designation + assessor eligibility |

## Open Access and Sustainability 

The Absolute Zero Academy is built on the principle that essential cybersecurity knowledge should be accessible. To ensure the long-term sustainability, continuous updates, and validation integrity of the Academy, we distinguish between open-access learning and formal credentials:

* **Open Curriculum:** Educational frameworks, study documentation, and foundational objectives are openly licensed and accessible to individuals and organizations for self-study. See the [AZ Foundations Learning Objectives](objectives.md) — published as a draft, open for feedback.
* **Services & Validation:** Official certification exam proctoring, formal credential issuance, and future instructor-led academy classes may include fees to cover platform scaling, administrative overhead, and rigorous identity verification.
